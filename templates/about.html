The Research Behind This Calculator

What We Found

A recent study analyzed mortgage performance and found that homebuyers who purchased in 2022 experienced significantly higher rates of payment problems compared to other years.

Key Findings:
  • Rising Interest Rates: 2022 was a transition year from low to high rates, leaving borrowers with higher payments and fewer refinancing options.
  • Peak Inflation: High inflation reduced purchasing power and increased living costs for borrowers.
  • Declining Savings: Personal savings rates dropped dramatically in 2022 after pandemic highs, reducing financial cushions.
  • Increasing Debt: Household debt levels surged, particularly credit card and student loan debt.

This calculator uses these research insights to help you understand whether current economic conditions might put you at similar risk.
